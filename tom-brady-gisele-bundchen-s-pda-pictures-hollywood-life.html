Tom Brady &amp; Gisele Bundchens PDA Pictures Hollywood Life

The way they were! Tom Brady and Gisele Bundchen were once one of the world’s top power couples, but some love just isn’t meant to last. The relationship ended in 2022 after 14 years of marriage, but they had quite the love story before that. Tom and Gisele got together in December 2006 after a mutual friend set them up on a blind date. Their relationship began just after Tom ended a two-year romance with Bridget Moynahan. Just two months into Tom and Gisele’s relationship, Bridget confirmed that she had gotten pregnant with Tom’s child before they broke up, and she gave birth to their son in August 2007. Despite the hiccup, Tom and Gisele’s relationship flourished, and they got married in February 2009. They went on to have a son of their own in December 2009, followed by a daughter in December 2012. While things appeared to be a fairytale romance, the relationship began to fracture in 2022 after Tom returned to football following a brief flirtation with retirement. Instead of hanging up his jersey, he decided to ditch his wedding ring. The couple finalized their divorce in Oct. 2022.
