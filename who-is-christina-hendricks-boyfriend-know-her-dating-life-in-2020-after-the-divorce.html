Who is Christina Hendricks Boyfriend? Know her Dating Life in 2020 After the Divorce

For more than 10 years, Mad Men actors, Christina Hendricks and Geoffrey Arend walked the red carpet as a power couple. Nearly a year back, however, the lovely duo announced they are walking their separate ways.

Since after the divorce, what's the relationship status of both of them? Are they again in the dating game? After countless joyful times and vowing to be together for the rest of their lives, the pair split. So, without further ado, let's find out more about Christina's romantic life after the divorce.

Is she Dating in 2020? Know her Boyfriend

Following her split with her former husband, the actress spends a wonderful time in Ashford Castle in Mayo. The castle is 800-year-old and Christina posed in her amazing Louis Vuitton and Burberry wardrobe.

Scrolling her Instagram and other social media accounts, the actress relationship status is single. She is taking her time off from the relationship and rediscovering herself. Just like the actress her ex-spouse spend a happy time in Pakistan, Istanbul, and The Netherlands.

'I was Like, 'Who is this guy?', Christina

Prior to her wedding with Geoffrey, the couple fell in love while filming together for Mad Men. As revealed by the paparazzi magazine, Cosmopolitan, Hendricks was impressed by Arend's striking personality. She said,

"Geoffrey walked in and I was like, 'Who is this guy?'... He just came in with this very high energy. He was just charming, funny, and very magnetic."

Half-way through the first season of Mad Men in 2007, Christina met her future husband Arend. Talking to Guardian, Hendricks recalls, the entire journey during the show changed their lives entirely.  

In October 2009, the duo decided to take their romantic affair into becoming husband and wife. The couple, however, do not share any children but they together raised two dogs.

Their Heartbreaking Divorce!!

Talking to People Magazine in 2009, the actress said how having the wedding ring on her finger makes her very happy. Hendricks realized that Geoffrey is the person she's chosen for the rest of her life. 

Well, their marriage did not exactly continue for the rest of their lives. On October 17, 2019, things between them changed and the duo decided to walk separate ways. Through an Instagram, the actress wrote,

"Twelve years ago we fell in love and became partners. We joined our two amazing families, had countless laughs, made wonderful friends and were blessed with incredible opportunities. Today we take our next step together, but on separate paths. We will always be grateful for the love we've shared and will always work together to raise our two beautiful dogs".

According to paparazzi magazines, the irreconcilable differences is the reason behind the split. As per the actress, the date of their separation is noted as April 19, 2019. She, furthermore, requested the court to terminate the ability to award spousal support to either party.
