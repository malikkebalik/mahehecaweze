Who Was Doreen Dougherty?

Doreen Dougherty was the principal of Holy Redeemer High School in Wilkes-Barre. She truly represented what it means to be a cherished mother, wife, and principal, always showing kindness and dedication. As a mom, she provided her children with love and care, making them feel safe and happy. As a wife, she understood deeply, stood by her partner, and faced life’s ups and downs together.

In her role as principal, Doreen was passionately devoted to education and helping her students grow in every way. She led with kindness, guiding young minds towards success and creating a place of respect and learning. Doreen managed to balance her family and school roles beautifully, making sure both her loved ones and the school community thrived.

Her wonderful ability to manage these different parts of her life left a positive impact on her family and the school community. Doreen will be remembered for her endless love, constant support, and inspiring leadership that touched the hearts of everyone she knew.

Obituary And Funeral Arrangements

The 8:00 a.m. Eucharist scheduled for this Friday, August 25, will not take place. This change is being made to get ready for the funeral of Mrs. Doreen Dougherty, who was the principal of Holy Redeemer High School in Wilkes-Barre. If you want to pay your respects, you can visit Holy Redeemer between 4:00 and 8:00 PM on Thursday.

There will also be a chance to see her in the church from 8:30 to 9:30 AM on Friday, followed by the funeral ceremony at 10:00 AM in the same church. We want to express our deepest sympathies to the Dougherty and Lipinski families, as well as to the Holy Redeemer community. Let’s hope she finds peace.
