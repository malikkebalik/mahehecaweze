Stripper and escort rakes in $34k per week catering to Wall Street

Explore More

Ho, ho, ho!

It’ll be a green Christmas for a former Wall Street accountant who reportedly rakes in $34,000 a week during the holiday season – as a stripper and escort catering to wealthy men looking for a “professional girlfriend.” 

Mia Lee, 35, gets her stockings stuffed with wads of cash mainly from private dancing sessions at one of Manhattan’s elite strip clubs, as well as by charging for luxurious getaways, she told The Post on Monday.

“A preponderance of my clients say I’m the most interesting woman they’ve ever met,” said Lee, who wouldn’t give up the name of her high-end jiggle joint.

“I have a combination of unique skills and interests and life experiences.”

A self-professed psychopath, according to Business Insider, Lee also competes in mixed martial arts and has a love for luxury items such as high-end cars, expensive liquor and imported cigars.

“I have traveled to over 60 countries, speak four languages, and have a slew of ‘tomboy’ hobbies like competitive shooting and racing,” she said.

The holiday season is not always the most lucrative, she added. This past June, Lee raked in $47,200 in one week.

But the big spenders tend to require her services more frequently toward the end of the year, resulting in her hefty weekly haul.

“I think it’s a combination of the cold weather and holiday parties that bring out more clients,” Lee told Business Insider.

“And a decent number of them don’t really like spending time with their families, so they’re looking for an escape.”

The one-time forensic accountant, who came to the US from China at a young age, revealed that she surprises her clients with her knowledge of financial affairs. She claims to have spent a dozen years working on Wall Street.

“I had a pretty colorful career in finance,” she told The Post. 

But when pressed for specifics, she would only say: “That’s for my clients to know.”

The sultry 5-foot-2 divorcee was in an open-marriage for three years that ended in 2021.”The negative externalities of the relationship were directly impacting my bottom line and that was completely intolerable for me,” Lee told Business Insider. “Anyone who f—s with my money, you are more than dead to me.”

She now markets her services for a pretty penny, according to her official website.

A client who wants her to stay overnight will have to cough up $7,000, while a four-hour rendezvous costs $4,000. The cheapest option is a minimum one-hour session that goes for $1,300.

Lee offers couples dates or same-day booking for an additional $500.

Wall Street jet-setters who want to whisk Lee away have to foot the bill for the flight, accommodations and ground transportation.

Domestic trips range from $7,000 to $12,000 and the price tag gets higher if they want to take her out of the country.

Trips to South America and Europe cost $20,000 at a minimum; and everywhere else is at least $30,000.

Aside from earning cash as a stripper and escort, Lee is the creator of her own YouTube channel called “Money Talks with Mia Lee” in which she offers financial advice.

She readily admits that her psychopathic tendencies enable her to shut off any empathy — which means she can focus on maximizing her time and money-earning hours while enjoying herself.

Lee also spends significant sums of money to maintain her appearance — including reportedly $10,000 for removal of all of her body hair, $100 every three weeks for eyelash extensions and $3,900 for miraDry, a sweat-eliminating treatment that mitigates the need for constant showering.

“Escorting is touch and go,” she told The Post. “It all goes up and down.”
