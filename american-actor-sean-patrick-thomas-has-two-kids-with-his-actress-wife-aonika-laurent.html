American Actor, Sean Patrick Thomas Has Two Kids With His Actress Wife, Aonika Laurent

Sean Patrick Thomas is an American actor who is famous for his role Derek Reynolds in the film Save the Last Dance in 2001. Further, he has appeared as Jimmy James in the film series, Barbershop and as Ronald Clifford in Cruel Intentions.

Thomas moreover starred as Detective Temple Page in the CBS TV series, The District from 2000 to 2004. Today we are exploring more interesting details about him so stick with us. 

Who is Sean Patrick Thomas Married Too?

Sean Patrick Thomas is a happily married man. He has tied the knot with his actress girlfriend, Aonika Laurent on Saturday, April 22, 2006, in a private wedding ceremony.

Their big day took place in New Orleans in the presence of their loved ones. Despite this, their wedding date was originally set for November 5, 2005, but it had to be rescheduled due to Hurricane Katrina.

The couple, who are both 50, first met at a party organized by Tim Story, the director of Barbershop. Ever since they shared a loving relationship and post photos of each other on their social media accounts on a daily basis.

On April 23, 2021, the 1.73 m tall Thomas and Laurent have celebrated their 15th year of marriage anniversary. "A French Quarter parade 15 years ago," he wrote on his Instagram account.

Sean Thomas has amassed a sizable fortune working in the film industry since 1995. According to Celebrity Net Worth, his net worth is estimated to be around $1 million which is same as Kario Salem.

As per his salary, Patrick makes $58,580 per year. One of his notable roles came after starring in The District as Det. Temple Page from 2000 to 2004. Further, he has had recurring parts in many TV shows like Lie to Me, Ringer, NCIS: New Orleans, Criminal Minds, The Good Fight and more.

Besides, Sean starred as Professor Macalester in the animated web series, Vixen from 2015 to 2016. 

Bio and Early Life

Sean Patrick Thomas was born on 17 December 1970 in Washington, D.C., United States. He is the son of Carlton Thomas, an engineer and Cheryl Thomas, a financial analyst for DuPont.

Patrick has two younger siblings. He graduated from Brandywine High School and later studied at the University of Virginia. Later, he enrolled at New York University's Tisch School of the Arts graduating in 1995. 

As per his citizenship, he is American and is of Afro-Guyanese ethnicity.
