Megan Kelly Early Life and Education

Born on November 18, 1970, in Champaign, Illinois, Megyn spent her formative years navigating different places, including Albany, Chicago, Rye, and Washington D.C., before settling in Manhattan. With a blend of German and Italian ancestry, Megyn holds American nationality and is rumored to adhere to the Catholic faith.

Megyn’s educational journey shaped her intellectual prowess. She commenced her schooling at Tecumseh Elementary School in Syracuse, New York, before her family’s relocation to Delmar, New York. Here, she pursued her high school education at Bethlehem Central High School.

In 1992, Megyn earned her undergraduate degree from the Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs at Syracuse University. Three years later, she achieved her Doctor of Jurisprudence degree from Albany Law School in 1995, solidifying her foundation for a career that would unfold in unexpected ways.

Megan Kelly Career Trajectory

Megyn’s professional journey embarked from the legal arena. Initially, she practiced law at Bickle and Brewer LLP in Chicago, followed by a notable stint at Jones Day, where she honed her legal expertise for nine years. However, destiny had different plans for her.

In 2003, she transitioned to the world of journalism, starting as a general assignment reporter at WJLA-TV. The turning point occurred in 2004 when she became a correspondent in Washington D.C. for Fox News. Her fearless commentary and engaging style propelled her to fame, leading to the creation of her show, “The Kelly Files.”

Megan Kelly Personal Life

Megyn Kelly’s personal life adds depth to her public persona. Born to Edward and Linda Kelly, she grew up in a Catholic Christian household. Tragedy struck at the tender age of 15 when she lost her father to a heart attack. Her family includes two siblings, Pete Kelly and Suzanne Crossly.

In the realm of relationships, Megyn is currently married to Douglas Brunt, an American novelist and former president of “Authentium,” a cybersecurity firm. The couple tied the knot in 2008 and shares two sons, Thatcher Bray Brunt and Edward Yates Brunt, along with a daughter named Yardley Evans Brunt.

Prior to her marriage with Douglas Brunt, Megyn was married to Daniel Kendall, an anesthesiologist. The union began in 2001 but concluded in divorce in 2006. This facet of her life showcases resilience and growth amid personal challenges.
