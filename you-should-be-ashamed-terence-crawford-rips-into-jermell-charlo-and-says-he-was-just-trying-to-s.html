'You should be ashamed' - Terence Crawford rips into Jermell Charlo and says he was just trying to s

Terence Crawford was not impressed with Jermell Charlo's efforts in the ring on Saturday night against Canelo.

The pair clashed in Las Vegas and the Mexican put in a dominant performance to drop Charlo in round seven on his way to a comfortable unanimous decision victory.

And in the aftermath, undisputed welterweight world champion, Terence Crawford, has accused Charlo of not even trying to beat Canelo.

"Jermell Charlo, you went out sad," Crawford tweeted after the fight.

"Didn’t even try to win, all you did was try to survive. You should be ashamed of yourself."

Before adding: "Congratulations Canelo you made the so called lion look like a baby cub."

But, we could see Crawford and Charlo in the ring soon, as in his post-fight interview, the American said he wanted to return to 154 lbs, having jumped up two weight classes to take on Canelo, and that he is going to push for a clash with 'Bud'.

"I wanna fight Terence Crawford," Charlo said. "I can fight Terence Crawford in my weight division. I'll fight Terence Crawford. I'm waiting."

But, after watching Charlo fight Canelo, Crawford revealed that he is no longer interested in the fight.

"Ok y’all I’m over Jermell Charlo he’s no longer on my hit list," Crawford said.
