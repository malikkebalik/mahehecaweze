Shotzi Blackheart Wikipedia and Biography

Shotzi Blackheart Wikipedia: -Shotzi Blackheart was born on March 14, 1992, in Santa Clara County, California, U.S. Shotzi Blackheart is an American professional wrestler and singer. She is signed to WWE, where she performs on the SmackDown brand under the ring name Shotzi. She is also well known for her time in NXT, where she was an NXT Women's Tag Team Champion. Prior to joining WWE, she performed for many promotions, including Shimmer Women Athletes, Evolve, Rise Wrestling, and Shine Wrestling, where she won the Shine Nova Championship. Shotzi Blackheart Career

Shotzi Blackheart Career: - In 2014, Urbanski debuted as Missy Highasshit, a cheerleading valet for the Stoner Brothers and stable Stoner U (a satirical spoof of Missy Hyatt and the Steiner Brothers) at Hoodslam in Oakland, California.  She also wrestled for All Pro Wrestling, Bar Wrestling, and Big Time Wrestling on the California independent circuit, as well as Nashville, Tennessee promoter Impact Wrestling. Under her true name, Blackheart competed in the sixth season of WWE's Tough Enough competition in 2015. She was initially chosen as one of six women to compete in that season's competition, but she had to withdraw before it began owing to an undetected irregular heartbeat that caused a failed medical. In May 2016, she tried out for WWE Performance Centre.

Shotzi began feuding with Damage CTRL (now comprised of Bayley and Iyo Sky due to Dakota Kai's injury) in the summer of 2023. Following an attack, they chopped some of Shotzi's hair. She then chose to retaliate by completely removing her green hair in a section, which was also done as an homage to her real-life sister, who is suffering from cancer.
