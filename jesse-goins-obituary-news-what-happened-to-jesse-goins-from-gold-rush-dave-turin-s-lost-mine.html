Jesse Goins Obituary – Cause of Death: What happened to Jesse Goins from Gold Rush: Dave Turin’s Lost Mine?

Jesse Goins, a beloved member of GOLD RUSH: DAVE TURIN’S LOST MINE died suddenly while working at Box Creek mine in Colorado on August 18, 2020. He was 60 years old.

Midway through the first season of Lost Mine, Jesse joined Dave’s operation and stayed on as the gold room operator with the team into the second season. The longtime miner brought Dave Turin to Grasshopper Creek, Montana, in 2018, and they partnered.

A gold rush or gold fever is a discovery of gold—sometimes accompanied by other precious metals and rare-earth minerals—that brings an onrush of miners seeking their fortune. Major gold rushes took place in the 19th century in Australia, Greece, New Zealand, Brazil, Chile, South Africa, California, the United States, and Canada while smaller gold rushes took place elsewhere.
