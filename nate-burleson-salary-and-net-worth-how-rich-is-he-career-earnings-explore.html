Nate Burleson Salary And Net Worth | How Rich Is He | Career Earnings Explore

Nate Burleson, a former wide receiver, achieved immense fame and wealth throughout his illustrious NFL career. The former athlete, a talented wide receiver, made more than $33 million throughout his playing career.

Despite his retirement from football, he still makes a comfortable livelihood as a sports presenter or pundit. Let’s find out more about Nate Burleson’s net worth, income, and professional earnings.

Nate Burleson is a former professional American football player who now works as a sports analyst and TV personality. Burleson was born August 19, 1981, in Calgary, Alberta, and played wide receiver in the National Football League (NFL) for 11 seasons. Growing up, he wanted to go to the University of Washington, just like his father, Alvin Burleson, and brother, Kevin Burleson.

Unfortunately, he never had the opportunity, so he attended the University of Nevada, where he thrived as a wide receiver for the Wolf Pack. His notable football career began in 2003 when the Minnesota Vikings selected him in the third round of the NFL draft. Nate went on to have a great NFL career, having played for the Minnesota Vikings (2003-2005), Seattle Seahawks (2006-2009), Detroit Lions (2010-2013), and Cleveland Browns (2014).

During his active NFL career, he had over 5,500 receiving yards and 39 touchdowns, and he was known for his versatile pass-catching talents. Burleson resigned from football and became a sports broadcaster, co-hosting “Good Morning Football” on the NFL Network. His broadcasting career is currently booming at Paramount, where he co-hosts CBS Mornings and contributes football commentary to CBS’s The NFL Today and Nickelodeon.
