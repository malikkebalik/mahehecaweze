Raul Jimenez included in Mexicos 26-man squad for World Cup in Qatar

published on July 03, 2024
Wolverhampton Wanderers striker Raul Jimenez has been included in Mexico’s 26-man squad for the World Cup in Qatar.  The 31-year-old has not played for Wolves since their 0-0 draw against Bournemouth in August. He injured his groin during a pre-match warmup before his side’s Premier League encounter versus Southampton on September 3.  Jimenez has been hampered with injuries for the majority of the 2022-23 campaign and originally missed the start of the season for his club due to a medial collateral ligament injury in his knee. RZA Net Worth

published on July 03, 2024
RZA’s estimated net worth is $18 million. Robert Fitzgerald Diggs, better recognized as RZA, is a renowned American record producer, rapper, film score composer, and entrepreneur. He is most notably known as a leading member of the popular hip-hop group “The Wu-Tang Clan,” which included other prominent artists like Method Man, GZA, Ghostface Killah, and more. The group’s inaugural studio album, “Enter Wu-Tang (36 Chambers),” released in 1993, significantly impacted the hardcore hip-hop genre and left a lasting influence on New York hip-hop style.
