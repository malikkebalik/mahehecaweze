More trout coming to Meridian's Kleiner and Settlers parks thanks to Idaho Fish and Game

published on September 09, 2024
MERIDIAN, Idaho (CBS2) — The City of Meridian says anglers can expect more trout in both Kleiner Park and Settlers Park ponds in the coming weeks. Idaho Fish and Game will stock both the Kleiner Park and Settlers Park ponds the week of Nov. 6 and again the week of Nov. 27 with catchable-size trout.
