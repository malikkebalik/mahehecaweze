Jwoww Shore loves her doctor

Need a boob job? Tell ‘em Jwoww sent you!

That’s right, the busty “Jersey Shore” star is now the poster girl for a Long Island plastic surgeon and when you mention her name you will get a nice little price cut on any kind of cosmetic surgery, according to Radaronline.com.

Jwoww, whose real name is Jenni Farley, announced today the all-new partnership, which includes a free consultation along with the discount, with Long Island’s very own Dr. Stephen T. Greenburg.

“If you say Jwoww referred you, you will receive a free consultation and a discount on any procedure you get done as a result.”

She even went to bat for the Long Island doctor, saying that she enjoyed not only the results, but the experience as well.

“I am happy to say that not only did I like the overall experience and I am more than happy with the procedure results,” Farley wrote on her website.

Farley has recently been in the news after being slapped with a $700,000 lawsuit by former boyfriend Tom Lippolis, claiming she owes him for setting up nearly $40,000 worth of cosmetic surgery including a boob job, cellulite and fat reduction.

There was no word whether Dr. Greenburg was the doctor referred to in Lippolis’ suit, but it does mention that Lippolis arranged the procedures for free in order for the performing physician to say he was Jwoww’s doctor.

Farley, who is known for her busty figure, has had two breast augmentation surgeries and knocks those who disapprove of her body.

“They look big,” Jwoww has previously said. “But I have such a wide upper frame that they actually are in proportion.”

The third season of the “Jersey Shore” premieres on January 6th on MTV.
