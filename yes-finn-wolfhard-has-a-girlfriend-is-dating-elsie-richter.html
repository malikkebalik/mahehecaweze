Suffers From Anxiety Disorder

Finn Wolfhard may be rich and successful but he doesn't have an easy life, thanks to his anxiety disorder. He first found out about it while he was filming for seasons three and four of Stranger Things. 

Elsie Richter's partner Finn has suffered a lot because of his anxiety issue. As of now, he is going through medication, consulting therapists, and even meditation to deal with it. Now, Finn tries to help those who struggle with the same anxiety problem as him by spreading awareness. He has said that he doubted if he would even have a career in acting due to this but he made it. He now tries to inspire other people.

Is Into Music And Has A Band

Wolfhard besides acting also has a passion for music. And, he not only sings as a hobby but as a profession as well. He has his music band called The Aubreys. However, he is more popularly known for his old band, Calpurnia.

Calpurnia was a short-lived band as it only existed for around two years, from 2017 to 2019. But, the band still has a huge fan base. Apart from Wolfhard, the band included Malcolm Craig, Ayla Tesler-Mabe, and Jack Anderson. The band found immediate success with its first song City Boy, making it to the top spot on Spotify's Global Viral 50 playlist.

Despite the success, Calpurnia disbanded as the band started heavily focusing and prioritizing commercial aspects over others. He then formed The Aubreys in 2019. The band was named after the cat he had when he was a kid. The band includes him and Craig from his previous band. Their song, No Offerings is their most streamed song on Spotify with over 3.7 Million Views.
