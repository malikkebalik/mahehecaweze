Photos Of Their Transformations Hollywood Life

The stars of 2000’s ‘Coyote Ugly’ have seriously gone on to have incredible, flourishing careers. August 4 marks the film’s milestone 20th anniversary, and we can’t help but look back at the stars of the beloved romantic comedy then and now! 

So many of the actors from the film have gone on to do incredible things, including Tyra Banks. After appearing in the film, Tyra went on to do amazing work in TV, including hosting her own show and appearing as a guest star on a number of TV series. She also hosted the wildly popular series ‘America’s Next Top Model’ for well over a decade.

Tyra’s model behavior and talent made her the perfect fit when ABC called and asked her to take over as the host for the 29th season of ‘Dancing With The Stars.’ To see more images of the stars of ‘Coyote Ugly’ and learn more about their work after the film, keep checking out the gallery by scrolling through the images below!
